About Me

Polymath: A person with extraordinarily wide-ranging knowledge.


My name is Annmarie Cong, an aspiring polymath. I am passionate about utilizing creativity and empathy to help others. I enjoy learning new things, developing an understanding to new things, and applying what I’ve learned to think of creative solutions to solve problems.


I am studying cognitive science and digital humanities at University of California, Los Angeles. Both of these studies are interdisciplinary studies that allowed me to learn a variety of technical skills and subjects– such as psychology, anthropology, philosophy, neuroscience, and computer science. During my spare time, I apply these skills to create design projects in hopes of solving problems that I see in my everyday life, and from creating these projects, I hope to learn something new everytime.


My Values...

COMPASSION


“Simply deal with matters with wisdom and treat people with compassion, without worrying about personal gain or loss. Then we'll never be plagued by vexations” — Master Sheng Yen

Compassion is the stepping stone to understanding another’s situation. It drives us to take action to give out a helping hand. Compassion is important in dissolving hate, injustice and ignorance. I believe that if one takes steps to try to become compassionate, they will be able to give a deeper meaning to everything they do.


HUMILITY


“With pride, there are many curses. With humility, there come many blessings.” — Ezra Taft Benson

Humility, or humbleness, is one of the important factors to self-improvement. By putting our ego and pride aside, it will allow individuals to recognize what they can improve on and allow them to see their own potential to grow.


PASSION


“The only way to do great work is to love what you do.” — Steve Jobs

I love seeing the passion people have in the things they do: it shows motivation, drive, and hard work. People with passion drives me to work even harder for the things I love and enjoy, and I hope I can spread the same energy to other people!


My Interests...

DESIGN


Design is ideating a creative solution for a problem. It is about the process that the designer goes through that inspires me to aspire to become a designer. The best thing about design is that this process is different for everyone and everyone can generate their own creative solution to a problem.


TECHNOLOGY


Technology has helped make lives easier for many people. As this world and people’s needs are ever-changing, I hope to be able to ease the gap of technology and people’s need.


CULTURES AND ART


As an Asian-American growing up in America, it was definitely hard to learn more about my Asian side. That is why I am always motivated to learn more about my culture, such as reading more about my history to learning how to write new Chinese characters! My goal is to be able to read and comprehend 3,000 Chinese characters!
